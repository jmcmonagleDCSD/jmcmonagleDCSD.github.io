DONATE TO SAVE BABY TURTLES

Billion Baby Turtles

We support sea turtle conservation organizations that work to protect sea turtle hatchlings on beaches worldwide. These funds pay local residents to patrol important nesting beaches, ensuring that the eggs are protected and the hatchlings reach the ocean. On average, we have helped save 10 hatchlings for every $1 donated.

 

How Billion Baby Turtles Works

Our grants help pay and equip local residents to patrol turtle nesting beaches, protecting turtles that come up to nest, and ensuring that the eggs are protected and the hatchlings reach the ocean. The funds are used to cover the costs of hiring local residents to patrol turtle nesting beaches and protecting the turtles and their nests. On average, we have helped save 10 hatchlings for every $1 donated. Learn more here.

COPROT has the vision of a bright and abundant future, where conservation projects find solutions with local stakeholders to protect the incredible biodiversity of the Osa Peninsula while also improving the quality of life for the community.  We hope that in this way we can develop a sustainable situation where humans and wildlife are able to live in harmony, and where we join forces to conserve and regenerate the incredible ecosystem here.

95% of donations made to COPROT through this page will be passed to the organization. 5% of the fees will cover credit card fees and administration.
