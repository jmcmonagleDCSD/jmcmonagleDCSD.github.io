Best Fishing Equitment

I would personally recomend a simple balsa float like the Thill and a larger South Bend Catfish Pole float. The Thill is perfect for Panfish of all kinds, and we own and fish them ourselves. They're simply amazing with worms, crickets, leeches, or wax worms on a hook beneath them.
