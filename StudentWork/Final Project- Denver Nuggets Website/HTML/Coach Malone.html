Coach Malone

Coach Malone is the official head coach of the Denver Nuggets. He helped bring them to their first championship in franchise history.

Head coaches in the NBA help to organize the team, their practice routines, and they are at the games cheering and giving advice. The coaches can also take timouts to stop the clock and talk with their team.

Coach Malone has coached everywhere from the knicks to the kings, he has helped the Nuggets build up their team, and win their first title.

Malone is very experienced, he has been coaching for 20 years.
