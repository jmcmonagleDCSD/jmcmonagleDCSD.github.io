Nikola Jokic

Jokic's Story

Nikola Jokic wasn't thought to be a superstar. He was drafted in the second round of the NBA drafting. Nikola Jokic was the start of the Denver Nugget's trek to become champions. He was the first star on the team. The Nuggets later acquired more amazing players, enabling them to win their title. Thoughout this journey, Jokic showed his team that he was one of the greatest basketball players of all time. That year, his stats were amazing and well-rounded, unlike many other all-stars.

The MVP and Superstar

Nikola Jokic has been the NBA MVP two times, then he skipped a year, now he is going for a third as of 2024. This superstar has been in the NBA for 9 years. All of these years include him being a team player, and an overall good basketball player. Even as a rookie, Jokic was extremely good. Nikola Jokic has gotten progressively better, he has lost wieght and practiced his shot, making him better than any other current basketball player.
