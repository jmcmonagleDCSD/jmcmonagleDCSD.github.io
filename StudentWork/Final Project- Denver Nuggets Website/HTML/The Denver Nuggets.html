The Denver Nuggets



The Denver Nuggets are Colorado's team in the NBA, the National Basketball Association. They have recently won a championship and all Denver Nuggets fans are hoping for another. This team has many exellent players. Each with his own specialty. The Nuggets have an amazing head coach, and even the bemch players are good.

This NBA team has a promising future and a golden destiny.

Image by: Rob Masefield from Flickr

An NBA dynasty is 4 championships. The Nuggets are hoping to reach this triumph. The players aren't the only ones who want this, the fans are desperate too. With improvement along the way, the Denver Nuggets are bound to reach this goal. Coach Malone started as the new head coach in 2015. Jokic was drafted this year too. Jamal Murray was drafted 2016. In the next five years, the team continued getting good draft picks, and building an amazing team.
