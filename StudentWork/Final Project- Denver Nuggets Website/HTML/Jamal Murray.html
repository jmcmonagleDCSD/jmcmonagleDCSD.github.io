The Beast Inside
When Jamal Murray wants to, he can unleash his skill in a flash.
Fans call this: a Murray Flurry.
In five or less minutes, Jamal can have scored at least three three pointers, and many other shots.

Jamal Murray is the second best player on the Denver Nuggets. He will soon make all-stars.
Jamal Murray is one of the best shooters in the league. He is also good at driving into the basket and scoring. He can do fancy moves while in the air, and on the ground. There are very few who can guard him when he is trying his best.

Why Jamal Murray is a Valuable Player

  • Jamal Murray is enthusiastic about basketball.
  • He is a good shooter.
  • He is a good clutch player.
  • Murray can take it to the hoop and score.
