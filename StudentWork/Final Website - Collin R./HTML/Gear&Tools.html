Gear & Tools

There are many tools and gear inside of beeswarm, and you might have to grind to get them. You start out with a shovel and a pouch, and you have to slowly get honey and materials to buy various different tools and gear. To start, lets look at early game, mid game, and end game tools and gear.

Earlygame Gear and Tools

  • Honey Dipper
  • Red or Blue Backpack
  • Basic Boots
  • Basic Helmet
  • Red and Blue Gaurds from the red and blue HQ
  • Belt bag or Mondo Belt Bag

Midgame Gear and Tools

  • Honey/bubble/fire mask
  • Porcelain Dipper or Petal Wand
  • Porcelain Backpack
  • Cobalt & Crimson Gaurds
  • Honeycomb Belt
  • Beekeeper Boots

Endgame Gear and Tools

  • End Game Dippers (Tide Popper, Dark Sycthe, Gummy Baller)
  • End Game Mask(Diamond, Demon, Gummy)
  • Coconut Canister
  • Coconut Clogs
  • Petal Belt
  • Cobalt and Crimson Gaurds

What will these things do?

The further you progress in the game, the harder it will be to get these items. But, the further the progress, the more pollen you will get from these tools and gear. This means that, the more pollen you collect, the petter your gear and tools are.
