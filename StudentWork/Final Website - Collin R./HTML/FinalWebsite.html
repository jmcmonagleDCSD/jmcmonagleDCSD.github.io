Bee Swarm Simulator

What is Bee Swarm Simulator?

Bee Swarm Simulator is a game on roblox made by developer Onett. Onett was inspired by another game on roblox called "Snow Plow Simulator"


Bee Swarm Simulator is a simulator type game on Roblox where you get pollen and turn it into honey. You can get pollen with many ways being your bees, tools, or passive gear abilities. Honey will then be used to get more bees, better gear, or many other things. You will also have to got to many differnt feilds to get pollen and complete quests.

Sections

The sections that are being discussed are Bears & Questlines, Bees, Gear & Tools, and feilds. Bears and Questlines give many rewards, Bees help gather pollen and convert pollen into honey, Gear and Tools help gather pollen and give buffs, and Feilds are where you get all of the pollen from.
