House Plants

even though we cant have most plants in are homes, but there are a few plants we can have in are home.


Dracaena trifasciata (Mother-in-law's tongue, or snake plant)

Dracaena trifasciata or Snake Plants are Great house plants, some of the benefits of snake plants are, Air purification, Remove toxic Pollutants,

And better sleep.

How to take care For a Dracaena trifasciata

Snake Plants are really just big succulents.Snake plant onbly need to be water 1-2 times a week,

and Allow soil to dry between waterings, to pervent root rot.

Snake plants can tolerant of a variety of light conditions, although indirect light is recomended.

Snake plants are NOT pet friendly, Dracaena trifasciata harm cats and dogs if ingested.
