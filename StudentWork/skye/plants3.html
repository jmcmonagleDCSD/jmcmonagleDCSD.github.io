What are venus fly traps? Venus fly traps carnivorous plants, they can live up to 20 years in the wild.

Venus fly traps are great for keeping bugs and other insects out of the house,

How to Grow and Care for a Venus Flytrap

Venus flytrap prefers a temperature of 70°F-95°F, although some varieties can survive 40°F in the winter with protection.

For the best Venus flytrap care, keep the environment humid.

Good air circulation is also important when growing Venus flytrap plants, so turn on a fan in the room where you keep them.
